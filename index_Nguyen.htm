Kanye West

Kanye West is one of the most famous musicians of the 21st century and he is one of the best selling artists of all time with more than 32 million albums and 100 million digital downloads worldwide. He has won 21 Grammy Awards which also makes him one of the most awarded artists of all time. He is also a fashion designer with his own clothing line and collaborations with Adidas. Kanye West is married to Kim Kardashian and has 2 children, Saint West and North West.

Flume

Harley Edward Streten or Flume is a 25 year old Australian record producer, musician and DJ. In 2012, his debut album 'Flume' was done on his first ever laptop and it was on the top charts and reached the double-platinum goal in Australia. His second album 'Skin' was released in May 2016 and is also nominated for 2 2017 Grammy Awards.

Childish Gambino

Donald Glover or Childish Gambino has a wide range of talents. He is an actor, writer, producer, director, comedian, rapper, singer, and songwriter. He first came into light when he was 23 and was hired to become a writer for the show 30 Rock. Later he played Troy Barnes on the NBC show Community and now he stars in the FX series Atlanta which he created.

Daft Punk

Daft Punk is a French electronic music duo formed in 1993 by Guy-Manuel de HomemChristo and Thomas Bangalter. The duo wear helmets and gloves to look like robots in most of their public appearances since 2001 and they rarely do interviews or appear on T.V. They also composed the score for the Disney film Tron: Legacy.

Bastille

Bastille are a British indie pop band formed in 2010. The name comes from Bastille Day. Their first album Bad Blood was released in March 2013 and entered the UK Albums Chart at #1. As of November 2014 Bastille have sold over 5 million records in the US and 2.5 million records in the UK.
